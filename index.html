About Me

I'm Zhuoran Chu 褚卓燃, a composer transitioning from concert music to film and game scoring. My work spans concert pieces, film scores, and interactive media. I'm also exploring how AI and emerging technologies can expand the possibilities of music production.

Currently studying at Eastman School of Music under mentors including David Liptak, Carlos Sanchez-Gutierrez, Tom Schneller, Hummie Mann, and Mark Watters.
